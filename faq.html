  • faceHow do I use the box models?
    The box avatars come with animations that replace the finger gestures (because the avatars haven't got fingers). What animations are available and which finger gestures they map to are listed on the pedestal.
    Desktop users can trigger these animations by holding left shift or right shift and using F1-F8. Vive users can trigger the animations by swiping on the touchpad in the direction of the animation you wish to trigger, then holding the trigger to "lock" the animation. I do not know how Oculus or Windows Mixed Reality users trigger animations; sorry.
    Note that you should only use one hand at a time to trigger these animations, or Unity will try to mix them together, leading to some horrifying faces. I do not recommend using two facial overrides at once.
    These are hard to get a hold of, so I highly recommend practicing in the mirror.
  • bug_reportI've uploaded one of the box models, but the animations aren't working!
    VRChat really doesn't appreciate avatars without fingers, and a lot of things break if your avatar doesn't have them, like the nubs on the box avatars. To fix this, in Blender, add invisible, non-weighted fingers at the end of the wrists, then set them in Unity. Problems solved (hopefully!)
